Privacy Policy

Your trust is important to us

At IKEKEVE MARTIA RETAIL, we are committed to respecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you shop with us online or in-store.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Such as your name, phone number, email address, and shipping address.
  • Order Details: Products purchased, payment method, and transaction records.
  • Website Usage: Information about how you interact with our website, including device type and browsing activity.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and deliver your orders efficiently and accurately.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To improve our products and services based on your feedback and shopping experience.
  • To send important updates, such as order confirmations and delivery notifications.

Protecting Your Information

  • Your personal data is stored securely and is only accessed by authorized team members.
  • We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
  • We do not share or sell your personal data to third parties, except as required to fulfill your order or comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications by following the instructions provided in our messages.

Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your shopping experience and analyze website traffic.
  • You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect immediately.
