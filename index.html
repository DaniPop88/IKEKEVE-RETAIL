About Us
Founded in 2020, IKEKEVE MARTIA RETAIL has been serving the Quezon City community with high-quality essentials and unmatched customer service. We are proud to be a Filipino-owned business, offering a wide range of products to cater to all your household needs.

Our mission: To provide affordable, reliable, and high-quality essentials that improve your everyday life.
  • Customer First: We are dedicated to serving you with a smile and making every shopping experience convenient and pleasant.
  • Quality Products: We only offer the best products from trusted brands to ensure that you get the most value for your money.
  • Community Commitment: We believe in giving back to our local community through excellent service and active engagement.
